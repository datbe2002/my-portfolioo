[ 07 / FAQ ]
Common questions.
Before you reach out
01 How long does a project take? +
It depends on scope. A landing page takes 1–2 weeks. A SaaS MVP with auth, billing and core features takes 4–8 weeks. I always define a clear scope and timeline at the start so there are no surprises.
02 What's your process? +
Discovery call → scoping & proposal → design (wireframes or Figma) → development in short iterations → testing & QA → deployment. You get a working build to review after every iteration, not a big reveal at the end.
03 How much does it cost? +
Pricing is project-based, not hourly. Every project is scoped individually — reach out with a brief and I'll come back with a fixed-price proposal. No vague hourly estimates, no scope creep surprises.
04 Do you work with international clients? +
Yes — I work fully remote with clients worldwide. Comfortable in English (TOEIC 880). I'm UTC+7 and adapt my schedule for async or sync collaboration with European and US time zones.
05 What do you need to get started? +
A brief description of what you want to build, who it's for, and your target timeline. That's enough to have a productive first call. No need for a spec document upfront — we'll figure that out together.
